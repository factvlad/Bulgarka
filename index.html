About Us

Dreams
Come True

Every city dweller dreams of a small country house, perhaps a dacha, so that there is an opportunity to take a break from the bustle of the city, to be in the fresh air and not to rush anywhere. So we decided to make our dream come true and began to equip our Gest House Bulgarka.

It started in 2019, it was a small house in which we put our souls, and now we continue to grow, we expand, we improve our territory in order to delight our guests and make their stay more comfortable. Let's get to know each other better: we are Alexandra and Yanko, and a piece of our heart is invested in everything that you see around. We are always happy to cook you fresh, tasty, farm food, with author's notes.

What We Do

Discover Our Services

Hot Jacuzzi

Hot Jacuzzi is perfect for relieving muscle tension after a hard day's work or workout. Warm bubbling water promotes thoughts of something good and allows you to completely relax.

Local Food

For us, this is not only a fashion trend, but an opportunity to feed our guests with delicious products, because we are in a place where there is everything for salad, dessert, and even mint in your cocktail will be from the garden.

Bar & Drink

We are not a bar with a strict menu, our bartender will prepare any cocktail for you (if the ingredients are available), and also add an author's touch with your permission.

Wifi-Zone

We have wi-fi, but we would very much like you not to use it - better lie on the grass, have a picnic, because what could be better - than live communication!?

Pet Friendly

We definitely need to take your beloved pet with us, of course, we hope that he will be friendly with our pets, because both cats and dogs live on our territory.

Playroom

We are also glad to see your children, we have a playroom with various toys (puzzles, balls, Lego, felt-tip pens and pencils) - we are sure that there will be something to keep your child busy.

Table Tennis

If you get bored with passive rest, you can warm up by playing table tennis.

Pool Table

We do not claim to be a gaming club, but the presence of a billiard table is unlikely to leave anyone indifferent, even if you did not know how to play billiards - this will be a great opportunity to learn.

Swimming Pool

It was not our plans to surprise with the presence of a pool, but we would like to even find it far from the sea, you had the opportunity to swim a little.
